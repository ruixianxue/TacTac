# TacTac Privacy Policy

This is the source text. A ready-to-host version is in `privacy-policy.html`.

## How to publish (required — Apple needs a reachable URL)

Easiest free option (GitHub Pages):
1. Create a new **public** GitHub repository, e.g. `tactac-privacy`.
2. Add `privacy-policy.html` to it (rename to `index.html` if you want a clean URL).
3. In the repo: **Settings → Pages → Build and deployment → Source: Deploy from a branch → Branch: `main` / root → Save**.
4. After ~1 minute your URL is live:
   - `https://<your-username>.github.io/tactac-privacy/` (if renamed to `index.html`), or
   - `https://<your-username>.github.io/tactac-privacy/privacy-policy.html`
5. Paste that URL into App Store Connect → your app → **App Privacy → Privacy Policy URL**, and also into the app's metadata "Privacy Policy" field.

Any public host works (your own domain, Notion public page, etc.) — it just has to load without a login.

Remember to replace the contact email placeholder below with a real address.

## Policy text

**Effective date:** 2026-07-01

TacTac ("the app," "we," "us") is designed to protect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

### The short version
TacTac does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. Everything you save stays on your device.

### Information you enter
When you tell TacTac where you put an item (by voice through Siri or by typing), the app stores the item name and the location you describe. This information is saved **locally on your device** using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). It is not uploaded to us or to any third party.

### Language understanding
TacTac uses Apple Intelligence (the on-device Foundation Models framework) to understand your sentences and phrase answers. This processing happens **entirely on your device**. Your words are not sent to our servers or to any external service.

### Location
If you grant location access ("While Using the App"), TacTac uses your device's location only to tag a saved item with one of your saved places (such as Home or Work). This is optional — the app works without it. Location data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

### What we do NOT do
- We do not have accounts or logins.
- We do not run analytics or advertising SDKs.
- We do not track you across apps or websites.
- We do not sell or share your data.
- We do not send your data off your device.

### Data retention and deletion
Because your data lives only on your device, you are always in control. You can delete any saved item or place inside the app at any time. Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.

### Children's privacy
TacTac does not collect data from anyone, including children.

### Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
